The Real Chicago Deep-Dish Pizza Dough

The Real Chicago Deep-Dish Pizza Dough is a buttery, flaky crust that's achieved by using corn oil (not butter) and minimal mixing and kneading times.

Ingredients

  • 2.25 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1.5 teaspoons white sugar
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour

Directions

Step 1

Dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water in a bowl. Let stand until yeast softens and begins to form a creamy foam, 5 to 10 minutes.

Step 2

Combine yeast mixture, flour, corn oil, and kosher salt in a large stand mixer fitted with the dough hook.

Step 3

Form dough into a ball and transfer to a buttered bowl. Turn to coat with butter, then cover the bowl with a towel and let rise in a warm place until doubled in volume, about 6 hours.
