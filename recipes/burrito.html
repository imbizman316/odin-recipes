Ingredients

  • 6 frozen bean and cheese burritos
  • 1(10.5 ounce) can condensed cream of chieckn or cream of mushroom soup

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 350 degress F (175 degress C).

Step 2

Place burritos in a row across the bottom of a 9x13- inch baking dish. In a medium bowl, stir together condensed soup, sour cream, and chile peppers.

Step 3

Bake for 25 to 30 minutes in the preheated oven, until cheese is slightly browend and burritos are heated through.
